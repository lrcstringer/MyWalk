MyWalk App

Privacy Policy

Effective date: 10 April 2026  ·  Last updated: 10 April 2026

MyWalk is a personal discipleship and habit app. Your spiritual journey is private. This policy explains plainly what we collect, why, and how we protect it.

1. What We Collect

Account information. When you sign in with Apple or Google we receive your name, email address, and profile photo. We store these to identify your account.

Content you create. Journal entries, habit notes, prayer requests, scripture memorization items, and recovery path responses are stored in your account.

Usage data. Firebase automatically collects basic analytics (session counts, crash reports) to help us improve the app. We do not use advertising SDKs.

Device permissions. If you grant permission, the app accesses your camera and photo library solely to attach images to journal entries. These images are stored in your account only.

2. Encryption

All journal entries and habit notes are encrypted with AES-256 before being stored on our servers. No one — including us — can read them. They are literally between you and God.

Encryption keys are derived from your account credentials and are never stored in plain text.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and sync the app across your devices.
  • To deliver features like Prayer Circles, accountability partnerships, and scripture memorization reminders.
  • To send push notifications you have opted into (habit reminders, circle activity).
  • To maintain your subscription status.

We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for advertising. We do not share your personal content with third parties.

4. Shared & Community Features

If you choose to participate in Prayer Circles, content you post (gratitudes, prayer requests, encouragements) is visible to other members of that circle. You control what you share and can leave a circle at any time.

Accountability partnership activity is only visible to you and your chosen partner.

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. You can delete individual journal entries and habits at any time within the app.

To delete your entire account and all associated data, go to Settings → Delete My Account in the app, or visit our account deletion page. This permanently removes all your content, circle memberships, and account credentials from our servers and cannot be undone.

6. Third-Party Services

MyWalk is built on Google Firebase (authentication, database, storage, cloud functions). Firebase processes data on our behalf and is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

Sign-in is handled by Apple or Google. We do not store your Apple or Google password. Please review their respective privacy policies for details on how they handle authentication data.

7. Children

MyWalk is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy we will update the effective date above and notify you via the app. Continued use of MyWalk after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach us at hello@mywalk.faith.
