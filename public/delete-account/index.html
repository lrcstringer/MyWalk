Delete Your Account

You have the right to delete your MyWalk account and all associated data at any time. All journal entries, habit notes, prayer history, and media are permanently removed from our servers and cannot be recovered.

Option 1 — Fastest

Delete directly in the app

If you have the MyWalk app installed and can sign in, you can delete your account instantly from Settings:

  1. Open MyWalk and go to Settings
  2. Scroll to the bottom
  3. Tap Delete My Account
  4. Confirm in the dialog

Your account, all content, and all data are deleted immediately and permanently.

or
Option 2 — Email request

Submit a deletion request

If you no longer have access to the app, email us and we will manually delete your account within 7 days.

Request deletion by email
This cannot be undone. Deletion permanently removes your account, all journal entries, habit data, prayer history, scripture memorization progress, circle memberships, and any uploaded media. Your subscription is not automatically refunded — please cancel your subscription first if applicable.
Keep your account

Reset your data only

If you want to clear your habits, journal entries, and progress without closing your account, use Reset All Data in the app:

  1. Open MyWalk and go to Settings
  2. Scroll to the bottom
  3. Tap Reset All Data
  4. Confirm in the dialog

This removes all habits, journal entries, scripture memorization, circle memberships, and uploaded media. Your account and sign-in credentials are preserved.
